Abilities

List of most abilties I can use during combat

Primary Lotus

Primary Lotus is a very powerful ability. In fact, if I am successfully able to catch my opponent of guard, it can possibly kill them. Firstly, I bring the opponent into the air. This is done through throwing them, knocking them upward, or even just waiting for them to jump by themselves. After this, I can wrap my fake bandages around them, completely unarming them. Lastly, I grab them, turn them into a nosedive, and begin rapidly spinning, drilling them into the floor.

Eight Gates

The Eight Gates are a last resort, yet is still very powerful. I can open a gate by reaching deep within myself and unleashing a bunch of concentrated spiritual energy. Upon opening a gate, my strength, speed, and endurance will increase significantly. The side effects to this incredible ability, however, can be quite dire. The ability consumes large amounts of chakra, meaning that the longer I stay in the upgraded form, the higher risk there is to my body. Opening the Eighth Gate is a guaranteed death after a specific amount of time. However, if the circumstances require it, I will use this ability.

Drunken Warrior

This ability is relatively straightforward. Upon consuming any amount of alocohol, I will gain immense power. This means increasing my speed, strength, and reflexes to ungodly levels. However, using this ability also makes me completely unconsious. I will be unable to make any choices, and I also might attempt to fight my own squad members.
